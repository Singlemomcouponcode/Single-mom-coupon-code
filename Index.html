Blog — Being a Single Mom Should Be a Coupon Code

I was scrolling through Facebook one night when I saw a post from a single mom with three kids and a full‑time job. Her only vehicle had broken down, and she was asking if anyone could take a look at it — or if a mechanic could at least tell her what was wrong and waive the fee.

She had no partner in her life. The kids’ dad was absent. She worked over 60 hours a week. Her children took care of each other while she worked, and every night she laid her head down and cried — not because of how society has failed her, but because she felt like she had failed her children.

Then a man, who didn’t know her or her situation, commented: “Being a single mom isn’t a coupon code.”

No. I started Single Mom Coupon Code because being a single mom should be a coupon code — a reason for the community to rally, to give a break, to acknowledge how hard you’re fighting every day.
